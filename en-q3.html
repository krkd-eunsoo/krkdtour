Earth Plaster Studio

Earth plaster is an eco-friendly building method that can be used to finish walls. Instead of using chemical materials that are harmful to our health and the environment, here, we use natural materials such as clay, limestone, plaster, straw and sand. Students of Earth Plaster School will develop their own craft and trade which makes use of this eco-friendly method.
