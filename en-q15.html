Transition Learning Research Center

There are two places at Kurikindi Center with many books: the Books Commons on the 3rd floor, and the Transition Learning Research Center. The research center publishes the magazine ‘Skills for life’ with articles written by young people who research how to lead good lives. Five issues have been published so far, with the theme bicycle, sun, plastic, earth, and garden.
