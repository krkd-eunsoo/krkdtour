Transition Learning Research Center

There are two places at Kurikindi Center filled with many books: the Books Common on the 3rd floor, and the Transition Learning Research Center. The Research Center publishes the magazine Skills for Life with articles written and researched by young people on how to lead good lives. Five themed-issues have been published so far: bicycles, the sun, plastic, the Earth, and gardens.
