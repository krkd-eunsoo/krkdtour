Haja Production School

Haja Production School is an alternative school where learning is based on the themes of ecology, peace, and living together. Through disciplines such as the humanities, urban farming, art and media, and language, young people decide what makes a ‘good life’ and the life they would want to live. The school is open to all young people who have chosen to not enter the mainstream educational system.
