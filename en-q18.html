Haja Production School

Haja Production School is an alternative school where learning is based on the themes of ecology, peace and living together. Young people think about the ‘good life’ they would like to live and study humanities, urban farming, art and media and language skills. The school is open to all young people who have chosen not to enter mainstream education.
