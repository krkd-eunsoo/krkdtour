Mokshin Woodwork Studio

In Mokshin Woodwork Studio, we do not use refined or finely dried wood. Instead, we gather broken pieces of wood from our surroundings and make them into spoons, stools and other daily tools. It is a project that allows us to carefully observe the trees and the wood they provide, and thus gain respect for the living forest.
