Parkour

Parkour is a sport that makes use of the urban environment such as railings, stairs, and walls. It urges us to see our everyday surroundings in a different light, discovering new uses and transforming ordinary spaces into a space of adventure. Parkour is not about danger; rather, it is about keeping yourself safe by being attentive to the environment. It doesn’t matter if you are a small child, a teenager, or elderly— Parkour is open to everyone.
