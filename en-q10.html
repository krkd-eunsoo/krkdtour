Young Chef School

Young Chef School is an alternative culinary school where young people learn to become “Chefs that bake life”. Every weekday, they operate the lunch restaurant Matjum(‘Welcoming lunch encounter’) which serves the community in Seoul Innovation Park. The cooking studio that they use also has a special name which comes from the teachings of Donghak(‘Eastern Learning’): Yichunshikchun(以天食天) - “To eat the sky as sky”. It means that we ourselves and the things we eat are all part of the universe - there is no better or worse, only that we are the universe.
