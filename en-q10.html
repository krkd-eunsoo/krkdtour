Young Chef School

Young Chef School is an alternative culinary school where young people learn to become “chefs that bake life”. Every weekday, they operate the lunch restaurant Matjeom (“Welcoming lunch encounter”) which serves the community in Seoul Innovation Park. The cooking studio also has a special name which comes from the teachings of Donghak(‘Eastern Learning’): Yicheon-shikcheon(以天食天) - “We are the sky, and as the sky, we eat the sky.”. It means that we along with the things we eat are all part of the universe. We are all equal. We are the food we eat.
