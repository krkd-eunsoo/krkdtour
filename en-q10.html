Timeline of Work

The Timeline of Work is an infographic exhibition about the evolution of work from the Industrial Revolution onwards. At the center, you will see a list of historical events written in chronological order.

In 1811, angry workers who believed that machines were robbing people of their jobs started a movement to resist the adoption of industrial machines.
