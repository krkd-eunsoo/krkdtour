Books Commons

The Books Commons is located near the main entrance on the 3rd floor. The Korean name of the area is ‘Rolling Stone’. Rolling stones do not stay long in one place. Rather they roll here and there, and as the surface is shaved and smoothened the stones evolve into various shapes. The Books Commons hopes to become a space for diverse ideas to connect with each other.
