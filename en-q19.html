Kurikindi Story

One day the forest was on fire. The animals of the forest ran and struggled to escape the danger. However, a small hummingbird named Kurikindi rushed back and forth into the forest with a single drop of water to put out the fire. The other animals saw this and laughed at Kurikindi, saying that it was pointless to try. Kurikindi replied,

"I'm just doing what I can."
