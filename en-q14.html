Beyond Nuclear Energy

In Korea, there are 24 nuclear reactors in operation in four places, Yeonggwang, Uljin, Gyeongju and Busan. The amount of nuclear energy produced is fourth in the world but Korea ranks first in density. The young people of Kurikindi Center are learning about the ways to go beyond nuclear energy. They participate in parades to raise awareness on the need to shut down old nuclear energy plants and produce safe energy.
