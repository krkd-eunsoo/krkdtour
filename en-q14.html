Beyond Nuclear Energy

There are 24 nuclear reactors operating in four places in Korea: Yeonggwang, Uljin, Gyeongju and Busan. In the total amount of nuclear energy produced in the world, Korea is ranked 4th, but in terms of area density, Korea is ranked 1st. The young people of Kurikindi Center are learning about the ways to go beyond nuclear energy. They participate in parades to raise awareness on the need to close down old nuclear energy plants and produce safe energy.
